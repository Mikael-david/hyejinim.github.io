Hi, I am Hyejin and I am a UX Designer based on Seoul. I love to think deeply about why people behave the way they do, and how changes to their environments influence their actions. I thrive in creative problem-solving, studying human behavior, and thinking about how to use that information to inspire design. At the heart of my motivation is a commitment to human-centered design and to rigorous research.
