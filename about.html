Projects About

I am a user experience designer who has about three years of work experience at pxd, a design consulting company in Seoul, Korea.

My goal is to combine design, ethnographic, and technical methods to tackle challenging relationship issues between people living in our society and to improve the potentials of individuals who want to overcome their personal and environmental problems. More specifically, I am interested in designing personalized technologies to address the issues of information inequality and digital discrimination.
