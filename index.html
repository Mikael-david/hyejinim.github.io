Laika Back to the Earth

#GAME #JAVA

2D simulation game using Java. It has game design mode and play mode.

This project was inspired by the real story of Laika, the soviet stray dog who was sacrified for a space experiment and could not come back to the earth.

Player can save other dogs in the space and make Laika come back to home. I hope more people can be awared of the behind story of this game: animals' sacrifice and contribution.

I would like to give a positive lesson to players through this game. It does not require fighting and competeting against enemies but helping ohters to achieve the success.

Alarm Clock

#PRODUCTIVITY #BEHAVIORCHANGE

"How might we make the sleephead wake up when they have to do?"

This app presents how much time user has in a day depending on when user wakes up and start the day. By allowing user to figure that out, the app helps user to set the ideal wakeup time, to prevent doing habitual snoozes, and to motivate to start a day as user planned.

Wakeup time is not just a moment but it affects the whole day. I would like to enhance the awareness of people who used to be not punctual by presenting these information; so people might can wake up rather than do snooze and just sleep more when alarm is ringing. SEE MORE

Visualizing Nonlinear Narratives with Story Curves

#VISUALIZATION #NONLINEAR-NARRATIVE #VISUAL-PATTERNS

Nam Wook Kim, Benjamin Bach, Hyejin Im, Sasha Schriber, Markus Gross, Hanspeter Pfister
IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics, 2018, IEEE VIS (InfoVis)
Kantar Information is Beautiful Awards Longlist

A nonlinear narrative is a storytelling device that portrays events of a story out of chronological order.

Story curves visualize the nonlinear narrative of a movie by showing the order in which events are told in the movie and comparing them to their actual chronological order, resulting in possibly meandering visual patterns in the curve. We also developed Story Explorer, an interactive tool that visualizes a story curve together with complementary information such as characters and settings.

I am in charged with a part of designing representing data and tagging data for visualization. SEE MORE
