Grooovy: Easy Beat Maker

Portable Digital Djembe for the Novice of Musical Instrument



Personal (Team)
ROLE User Research, Interaction Design
DURATION Jan 2015 - Mar 2015
COLLABORATORS Hyunjoon Kwon, Intak Joo, Minyoung Yoo


Groovy is an easy-to-use and portable digital djembe, supporting customizable sounds with three di erent con gurations including EDM, modern drum, and traditional drum like djembe. It can recognize beats in background music and guide tap timings. My role in this project was to de ne user needs and develop appropriate interaction scenarios. We developed a working prototype using Arduino and 3d printing.
