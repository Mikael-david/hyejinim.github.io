Smart Home Products

Wall pad and mobile interface designs for a home automation app monitoring and controlling various devices



CLIENT Commax
ROLE Design Strategy, UX/UI Design
DURATION Jan 2016 - Apr 2016


We designed wall pad and mobile interface designs for a home automation app monitoring and controlling various devices. Our goal was to minimize redundant controlling tasks and maximize accessibility by providing useful and friendly information in di erent contexts. We investigated the needs of users in di erent situations and identi ed problems during the course of user actions. Our design focuses on providing context-aware information and revealing appropriate features in di erent moods and places. The deliverables include service journey maps and wireframes.
