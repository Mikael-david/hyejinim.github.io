Time Transcendence

Messenger for people who are in long-distance relationship



Personal
ROLE User Research, Design Strategy, UX/UI Design
DURATION Dec 2016


I started this project with the idea of supporting communication between people living in di erent time zones. People want to be connected with their beloved ones especially when they are separated in remote places. But sometimes they postpone sending a message or calling to avoid interrupting the others in di erent times, In addition, it is also di cult for them to keep aware of exact time di erences. My design addresses the issues with time- aware messages to help maintain healthier relationships.
